# MIDI sources

All five pieces are in the public domain (composers deceased > 70 years
or traditional). MIDI files sourced from the freely-distributed
[mfiles.co.uk](https://www.mfiles.co.uk/classical-midi.htm) archive,
which makes them available for non-commercial use and learning.

| File | Composer / origin |
|---|---|
| `01-ode-to-joy.mid` | Beethoven, Symphony No. 9 mvt. 4 (piano solo arrangement) |
| `02-pachelbel-canon.mid` | Pachelbel, Canon in D (5-part: 3 violins, cello, harpsichord) |
| `03-frere-jacques.mid` | Traditional French nursery round |
| `04-bach-prelude-c.mid` | J.S. Bach, WTC Book I, Prelude in C major (BWV 846) |
| `05-greensleeves.mid` | Traditional English folk song |

These files are consumed at build time by `scripts/generate-demo-scene.ts`
and woven into spatial scenes by the Museeka pipeline.
